Shredded Chicken Tacos

Description

These chicken tacos are perfect for Taco Tuesday and are a healthy choice for a variety of lifestyles!

Ingredients

  • 3 ibs chicken thighes
  • 16 oz salsa(a mango pineapple variety recomended!)
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 1 white onion, diced
  • 1 red bell peper, diced
  • 3 tbsp fresh cilanto,chopped
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • cumin, to taste
  • oregano, to taste

Steps

  1. In your slow cooker, mix the salsa, lime juice, taco seasoning, onion, bell pepper, and cilantro.
  2. Rub the chicken thighes with salt, pepper, cumin, and oregano, then mix into the sauce. Cover and cook on low for 7-8 hours.
  3. Remove the chicken, shred with two forks, return to the slow cooker and stir.
  4. Serve in either soft or hard shelled tortilla or over nachos, top with cheese, salsa, and/or sour cream as desired. Enjoy!
