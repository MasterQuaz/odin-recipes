Chili Rubbed Pulled Pork

Description

Pulled pork is very easy to make, especially if you have a slow cooker. Slow cooking holds in all the moisture during cooking and slowly will break down the connective tissue, so that the meat ends up so tender that it easily shreds apart with the touch of a fork. Chili Rubbed Pulled Pork is a tender, juicy, and flavorful meat that can be used for tacos, nachos, salads, and more!

Ingredients

  • 3 Ibs. boneless pork butt or shoulder
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp freshly cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine chili powder, smoked peprika, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Cut the pork into 2-3 inch cubes. Place the pork cubes in a large bowl, sprinkle the prepared spice mix over top, then stir to coat the meat in the spices.
  3. Place the seasoned meat in a slow cooker, place the lid on top, and cook on low for 8-9 hours.
  4. After cooking, stir the meat with a fork to shred the meat. If it's not tender enough to fall apart when stirred, return the lid and cook for an additional 30 minutes then try again. After shredding the meat, it is ready to serve!
